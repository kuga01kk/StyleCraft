Style Guide

Your reference guide to looking perfect for every shape & occasion

Occasion Styling

Wedding

Look your best for the special day. Opt for detailed embroideries, deep warm tones, or graceful pastel flowy silhouettes.

Office Wear

Smart, sleek & professional looks. Rely on solid colors, sharp lapels, structured pantsuits, and leather accessories.

Party Glam

Trendy & glamorous party outfits. Choose satin fabrics, bold accents, shimmering materials, and chic statement heels.

Casual Comfort

Comfortable & stylish everyday looks. Think cotton co-ord sets, graphic tops with white denim, and canvas shoes.

Body Type Styling

Pear Shape

Highlight your upper body with boat necks and cowl necklines. Choose A-line skirts to gracefully wrap your lower half.

Hourglass

Show off your natural balance and curves. Belted garments, wrap dresses, and sweetheart necklines suit you beautifully.

Rectangle

Create the illusion of curves. Wear cinched dresses, ruffles, round necklines, and bold patterns around hips and shoulders.

Apple Shape

Highlight your legs and collarbone. Choose flowy empire waist lines, structural jackets, and soft V-necks to elongate your torso.

Inverted Triangle

Add volume to your lower hips. Pair clean, simple tops with wide-leg trousers, flared skirts, and cargo style details.

Quick Fashion Tips

01

Shape Selection

Always pick clothes that complement your unique body shape to feel comfortable.

02

Color Harmony

Wear colors that match your skin tone. Warm tones suit gold; cool tones suit silver.

03

Classic Wardrobe

Invest in timeless pieces (white shirts, blazers) that never fade from style.

04

Accessorizing

Delicate accessories and matching bags can elevate even the simplest t-shirt & jeans.
