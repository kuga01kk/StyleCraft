Seasonal Fashion

Summer

Lightweight breathable fabrics like linen, chiffon, and organic cotton. Dominated by soft pastels, yellows, and white tones.

Monsoon

Water-resistant layers, quick-drying synthetics, and shorter hems. Dominated by deep lavender, slate blue, and teal highlights.

Autumn

Light jackets, soft cardigans, and overlay scarves. Dominated by warm earth colors like beige, rust, and olive green.

Winter

Woolen coats, velvet ethnic garments, and trench layers. Dominated by rich, bold tones like crimson red, navy, and emerald.
